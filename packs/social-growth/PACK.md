# Social Media Growth System

## Overview
A proven framework for growing a social media audience across Twitter/X, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Instagram using viral content mechanics and community building.

## What You Can Build
- Growing personal brand
- Community around a product
- Viral content pipeline

## Workflow Breakdown
1. **Audience Research** → Identify niche, ICP, and competitors
2. **Content Hooks** → Write scroll-stopping first lines
3. **Posting Strategy** → Optimal timing and frequency
4. **Community Building** → Engagement loops and DM funnels
5. **Analytics** → Track growth and double down on winners

## Outcome
An engaged social audience with a documented growth playbook.
