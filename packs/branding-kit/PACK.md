# Brand Identity Kit

## Overview
Build a complete brand from scratch. From market positioning and naming to visual guidelines, voice, and launch-ready marketing assets.

## What You Can Build
- Startup brand identity
- Personal brand system
- Product rebrand package

## Workflow Breakdown
1. **Brand Strategy** → Positioning, differentiation, ICP
2. **Naming** → Brand name ideation and validation
3. **Visual Identity** → Logo direction, color, typography
4. **Brand Voice** → Tone, messaging, writing guidelines
5. **Launch Assets** → Social bios, pitch one-liners, taglines

## Outcome
A complete, consistent brand identity ready for market launch.
