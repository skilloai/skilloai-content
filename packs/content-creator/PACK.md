# Content Creator Studio

## Overview
A full content production system for creators and marketers. Go from strategy to published content across every channel: blog, social, video, and email.

## What You Can Build
- 3-month editorial calendar
- Multi-platform content pipeline
- Automated content repurposing system

## Workflow Breakdown
1. **Content Strategy** → Pillar topics, audience mapping, calendar
2. **Blog Writing** → Long-form SEO articles with structure
3. **Social Content** → Platform-specific posts and threads
4. **Video Scripts** → YouTube, TikTok, and shorts scripts
5. **Newsletter** → Email sequences and digest formats

## Outcome
A consistent, multi-channel content engine that builds authority.
