# Indie Hacker Launch Kit

## Overview
Everything an indie hacker needs to launch fast: a high-converting landing page, SEO, email capture, social strategy, and analytics.

## What You Can Build
- Product landing page
- Waitlist campaign
- Pre-launch marketing system

## Workflow Breakdown
1. **Landing Page** → High-converting hero, social proof
2. **SEO Setup** → Meta, schema, sitemap
3. **Email Capture** → Waitlist + drip sequence
4. **Social Launch** → Twitter threads, Product Hunt copy
5. **Analytics** → Conversion tracking and funnels

## Outcome
A fully launched indie product with an active waitlist.
