# Product Growth Engine

## Overview
A systematic approach to product-led growth. Build viral mechanics, referral programs, and conversion funnels that compound over time.

## What You Can Build
- Viral referral program
- Onboarding conversion flow
- Retention system

## Workflow Breakdown
1. **Growth Loop** → Design viral mechanics
2. **Referral System** → Build invite-based growth
3. **Conversion Funnel** → Optimize signup to activation
4. **Retention** → Reduce churn with engagement hooks
5. **A/B Testing** → Continuously improve key flows

## Outcome
A self-sustaining growth engine built into the product.
